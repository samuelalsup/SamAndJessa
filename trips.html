August 2020

Big Sur Camping!

On August 1st, we drove up to Plaskett Ridge and had the time of our lives on the beautiful swing and enjoyed the warm summer day looking at the coastline. We spent the night in our tent and then went down to Pfeiffer Beach where we cooked the best breakfast I've eaten in my life in the parking lot there. Truly the best birthday celebration I've ever had! Can't wait for our next journey there together!
